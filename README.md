# 🌍 Our Mission

At **Sonrise Ministry**, we believe that faith is demonstrated through action.

Our mission is to serve communities by providing practical help, spiritual encouragement, and hope to those in need. Through acts of kindness, charity, education, and community support, we strive to reflect the love of Jesus Christ in everything we do.

---

# ❤️ Our Ministries

## 🍽 Food Distribution

Providing meals and essential food supplies to families and individuals facing hardship.

---

## 📚 Education Support

Supporting children and students through educational assistance, learning resources, and encouragement for a brighter future.

---

## 🤝 Community Outreach

Reaching communities through care programs, prayer support, counseling, and compassionate service.
